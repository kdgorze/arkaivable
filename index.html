About Me & My Art :0

hi, i'm kai. im a 23 y/o that loves to be creative where i can. i code for $ and make art when i can. being creative is my main outlet, as it always gives me something to look forward to.

i created "arkaivable" for a multitude of reasons. i love to be original, and i want everyone to know if i create something, it is 101% me. my art has tons and tons of easter eggs and meaning behind it. the longer you look, the more you find and the more random things start to relate together. i love to think of it as a big word search, or a maze even. growing up i watched my dad create art in his own way, and that truly still inspires me to create art how i do. i never care what others think of it, as i know my dad would be proud knowing i stayed true and original to myself.

as stated before, all art will always be original and created through me. my biggest strengths are creating my work off the dome and in the moment, letting random witty thoughts take the better judgement while i create.
